## Justin Day — Intake Form + Google Drive Workflow

Justin shared two workflows that dramatically improved output quality:

**1. Intake Form → Google Drive → Claude**
- Google Form collects all client info (brand, content, services, images)
- Give Claude/Warp access to Google Drive
- Reference business by name → Claude pulls live data from intake → output is specific, not generic

> *"By feeding that information somewhat seamlessly to the terminal, yeah, was a big help for me, too. You're not giving surface-level stuff. You're giving it the nuts and bolts."*

**2. Claude → Deep Research → Copy Pipeline**
- Built a branding skill (took 2 days)
- Branding skill outputs a detailed brand document
- Feed brand doc + competitors into deep research → extract Google Reviews insights → build copy
- Goal: string it all together into one automated pipeline

> *"I built a branding skill that took me like two days. Days to finish, and it pumped out this crazy cool document. And then I like to take that, have it do deep research using the branding document, their competitors, and go through all their Google reviews, and extract any deep listening insights on that, and take all of that, and then develop the copy using another prompt."*

**Google Tag Manager + other Google asset MCPs:**
> *"I learned very quickly that Google Tag Manager and a lot of the other Google assets have individual MCPs for certain sections. In my intake process I'm developing, I'm going to force everyone to use Google Drive and have them pre-set up, because MCP loves pulling from Google Drive."* — Justin
