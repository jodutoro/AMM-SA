## 2. Brand Identity & Values

Bella Caramella is not just a clothing label — it is an **identity stamp**. The brand exists at the intersection of fashion, self-expression, and community.

### Brand Promise
To offer not only the best quality and comfort in clothing, but also a space to share and be oneself with total freedom.

## 3. Target Audience

> **Founder insight (voice note):** "Initially the brand was very focused on youth fashion, but day-to-day in commerce I realize that all women, of any age, of all sizes — that's where we focus. From size S to XXL."

### Audience Overview
Bella Caramella's audience is **all women who feel proud of their femininity** — regardless of age or body type. The brand aesthetic skews youthful and sweet, but the product and size offering is deliberately inclusive.

### Primary Segment — Young & Active Women
- **Who:** Women approx. ages 16–32
- **Mindset:** Proud of their femininity, embrace their sweetness and personality
- **Lifestyle:** Fashion-conscious, highly active on Instagram and TikTok, discover brands digitally
- **Pain points:** Wanting clothes that feel *like them* — expressive, not generic
- **What they seek:** Style, quality, comfort, and emotional connection to a brand

### Secondary Segment — All Adult Women
- **Who:** Women 30+, of all body types (S–XXL)
- **Mindset:** Values quality, femininity, and a boutique feel over mass-market fashion
- **Lifestyle:** May discover through Instagram, but purchases primarily via WhatsApp referral
- **Pain points:** Hard to find feminine, quality pieces in their size without a generic feel
- **What they seek:** Inclusive sizing, curated selection, personal shopping experience

### Size Inclusivity
The brand carries sizes **S through XXL** — this must be visible and celebrated on the website, not buried in product details. Consider a dedicated size-inclusivity statement in the About section and on product pages.

### Buyer Personas

**Persona 1 — Valentina (Primary)**
Age: 22 · University student · Colombia urban · Instagram + TikTok daily · WhatsApp for purchases
*"Quiero algo que se vea como yo: bonito, divertido y cómodo."*

**Persona 2 — Marcela (Secondary)**
Age: 38 · Professional / Mom · Colombia urban or suburban · Discovers via Instagram, orders via WhatsApp
*"Quiero sentirme femenina y elegante sin sacrificar comodidad, y que realmente haya en mi talla."*

## 4. Visual Identity

### 4.1 Logo

The logo is composed of the brand name written in a script typography that faithfully reflects the brand's values. The initial "B" is adorned by a floral ornament that represents the youth and femininity the brand aims to embody.

#### Logo Versions

| Version | Description | Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Vertical** | Stacked "Bella" over "Caramella" with floral B | Primary, for square formats, tags, packaging |
| **Horizontal** | "Bella Caramella" side by side | Horizontal banners, headers, business cards |
| **Symbol — B** | Decorated "B" initial with floral ornament | App icons, watermarks, small applications |
| **Symbol — Flower** | Symmetric flower (mirrored ornament) | Decorative, pattern, iconography |

#### Two Core Symbols
1. **Floral B** — The letter "B" (brand initial) adorned with the floral ornament; represents the brand identity in compact form.
2. **Symmetric Flower** — The same ornament mirrored to create a geometric, symmetrical flower shape; used as a standalone decorative/iconographic element.

### 4.3 Typography

#### Primary Font — Tahu
- **Use:** Titles (H1, H2), navigation labels, signage, hero text, key highlighted words within copy
- **Character:** Script/handwritten, flowing, feminine, playful
- **Role:** High protagonist — carries the brand's personality in visual headlines
- **Web implementation note:** Load via self-hosted or licensed source; ensure fallback to a complementary cursive Google Font (e.g., `Dancing Script` or `Pacifico`) if Tahu is unavailable

#### Secondary Font — Ordinary
- **Use:** Body copy, longer text blocks, product descriptions, captions, form fields
- **Character:** Rounded, friendly, still informal but more legible at small sizes
- **Role:** Supports the primary font; maintains visual coherence without competing
- **Web implementation note:** Pair with fallback `sans-serif`

#### Typography Hierarchy (Website)

| Level | Font | Weight | Approx. Size | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | Tahu | Regular | 52–72px | Hero headlines |
| H2 | Tahu | Regular | 36–48px | Section titles |
| H3 | Ordinary | Bold | 24–30px | Sub-sections |
| Body | Ordinary | Regular | 16–18px | All body text |
| Caption | Ordinary | Regular | 12–14px | Image captions, tags |
| CTA Button | Ordinary | Bold | 14–16px | Buttons, labels |

#### CSS Font Stack
```css
--font-display:  'Tahu', 'Dancing Script', cursive;
--font-body:     'Ordinary', 'Nunito', sans-serif;
```

## 5. Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Pillars
- **Warm & Personal** — Speaks like a best friend, not a corporation
- **Playful & Fun** — Light humor, exclamation points, energy
- **Empowering** — Celebrates the audience, never talks down
- **Fresh & Current** — Uses youth language naturally, stays relevant
- **Radically Inclusive** — Every woman, every age, every size belongs here — the tone never excludes

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Product descriptions | Playful + descriptive | *"Este vestido fue hecho para brillar — y tú también."* |
| Campaign headlines | Bold + exciting | *"¡Espera lo nuevo!"* / *"Lo mejor del verano en un solo lugar"* |
| Social captions | Conversational + fun | *"¿Cuál es tu colección favorita? Cuéntanos abajo 🌸"* |
| Customer service | Warm + helpful | *"Hola linda, con mucho gusto te ayudamos..."* |
| Website copy | Inviting + confident | *"Descubre prendas que hablan por ti."* |

### Writing Rules
- Use second-person singular **(tú)** — direct and personal
- Favor short sentences and exclamations in headlines
- Use ellipsis (...) for suspense in teasers
- Exclamation marks are encouraged — this brand is joyful
- Avoid corporate language: no "estimada clienta," no "dicho producto"
- Spanish is the primary language

## 7. Website Architecture

> **Founder direction (voice note):** *"La idea de la página web es que sea tipo catálogo, no tipo venta web, porque al ser una boutique que manejo unidades... me gustaría que dirija a la gente directamente al WhatsApp para preguntar la disponibilidad."*

### Website Model: Catalog → WhatsApp

This is a **catalog website**, not an e-commerce store. There is no shopping cart, no checkout, and no inventory management system. The website's only conversion goal is to **get the visitor to WhatsApp** to ask about availability and place an order.

**Why this model fits Bella Caramella:**
- Boutique operates on single units — no guaranteed stock per size/color
- Inventory changes frequently and unpredictably
- The founder manages availability personally via WhatsApp
- Personal, warm boutique experience is a competitive differentiator

**Primary CTA on every page:** → `Consultar disponibilidad por WhatsApp`

### Key Website Pages — Design Notes

#### Home Page
- **Hero section:** Full-width gradient background (holographic), Tahu font headline, Bella Pink CTA button → WhatsApp
- **Tone of hero headline:** *"Viste lo que eres. Bella, libre, tú."*
- **Section background rhythm:** Alternate between white and soft blush (#f8d3ce)
- **No price display required** — prices are shared via WhatsApp; catalog can show product only
- **WhatsApp button:** Sticky floating WhatsApp button on all pages (bottom-right corner), Bella Pink color

#### Product / Catalog Pages
- Background: white or very light blush
- Product name in Tahu font, description in Ordinary font
- **No "Add to Cart"** — replace entirely with: `"Consultar por WhatsApp"` button (Bella Pink)
- Add availability note: *"Al ser una boutique, manejamos unidades limitadas. Consulta disponibilidad antes de tu pedido."*
- Show size range (S–XXL) even if specific availability is confirmed via WhatsApp

#### WhatsApp CTA Button — Recommended Message Template
Pre-fill the WhatsApp link with a message to reduce friction:
```
https://wa.me/[NUMBER]?text=Hola%20Bella%20Caramella!%20Me%20interesa%20el%20[PRODUCTO]%2C%20%C2%BFtienen%20disponibilidad%3F
```
Renders as: *"Hola Bella Caramella! Me interesa el [PRODUCTO], ¿tienen disponibilidad?"*

#### About Page
- Feature the brand manifesto from the manual
- Use the holographic gradient as a full-width decorative section
- Size inclusivity statement: *"Para todas. De la S a la XXL."*
- Show the two logo symbol variants decoratively

## 10. Logo Usage Rules

### Correct Uses ✅
- Original full-color version on white or light backgrounds
- Original negative (white logo) on Bella Pink or gradient backgrounds
- Black and white version for monochrome print
- Black and white negative for dark backgrounds
- Both vertical and horizontal orientations
- Symbol-only (Floral B or Symmetric Flower) for compact applications

### Incorrect Uses ❌
- **Wrong colors** — Never recolor the logo in green, blue, or any non-brand color
- **Deformation** — Never stretch, compress, or distort proportions
- **Incorrect typography** — Never recreate the logo with a different font
- **Incorrect spacing** — Never alter the spacing between the ornament and letterforms
- **Low contrast** — Never place the logo on a similarly-colored background without sufficient contrast
- **Shadows / effects** — Never add drop shadows, glows, or outlines not part of the original

### Minimum Size
- Logo (full): minimum 120px wide (digital) / 30mm (print)
- Symbol: minimum 32px / 10mm

### Clear Space
Maintain a clear space equal to the height of the "B" symbol around all sides of the logo.
